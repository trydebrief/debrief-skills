# /debrief recruit

Candidate scoring, interview debrief, pipeline status.

## Apps
- **Notion** — pipeline tracker, candidate pages, debrief storage.
- **Granola** — interview transcripts.
- **Gmail** — candidate correspondence.

## Step 1: Pipeline status
Use Notion to pull the recruiting pipeline (open roles, candidates per stage, stalled candidates, decisions pending).

## Step 2: Recent interview debriefs
Use Granola to find any interview in the last 48h without a written debrief. Draft structured feedback: signal on each competency, concerns, recommendation. Save to the candidate's Notion page.

## Step 3: Candidate scoring roll-up
For candidates with all interviews complete (per Notion), aggregate feedback into a go/no-go recommendation with reasoning.

## Step 4: Present
Grouped by role. Lead with decisions-needed. Link to Notion candidate pages.

## Guidelines
- Interview feedback must be evidence-based. Every claim needs a quote or specific moment from the Granola transcript.
- Don't aggregate across interviewers with a simple average. Weight by how directly each interviewer observed the relevant competency.
