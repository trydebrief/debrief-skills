# /debrief eod

Close the day: what got done, what slipped, what to carry to tomorrow.

## Apps
- **Google Calendar** — meetings completed today; conflicts and overruns.
- **Linear** — tickets closed and tickets due today still open.
- **Slack** — replies still owed. Also check saved-for-later via `is:saved`.

## Step 1: What got done
- Use Linear to list tickets you closed today.
- Use Google Calendar to list meetings completed (note any with recorded decisions).
- Note merged PRs / commits if connected.

## Step 2: What slipped
- Use Linear to list tasks due today still open.
- Use Google Calendar to flag meetings that got canceled or ran long.
- Search Slack for threads where the user owes a reply; include Slack's saved-for-later (`is:saved`) items.

## Step 3: Carry-over
Propose a short list for tomorrow's top-of-day. Surface anything with a hard deadline in the next 48 hours (Linear due dates, Calendar events).

## Step 4: Present
Keep it to one screen. End with: "Want me to draft tomorrow's plan now?"
