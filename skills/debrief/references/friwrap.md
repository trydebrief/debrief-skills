# /debrief friwrap

Week-in-review plus week-ahead, bundled. Run on Friday afternoon.

## Apps
- **Google Calendar** — meetings held this week and next week's load.
- **Linear** — tickets shipped, in flight, and stalled.
- **Slack** — commitments made and their status. Also check saved-for-later via `is:saved`.
- **Gmail** — major threads driven this week and outstanding replies.

## Step 1: Week in review
- Use Google Calendar to list meetings held and key decisions.
- Use Linear to list tickets shipped and PRs merged.
- Use Slack and Gmail to surface commitments made this week and their status; in Slack, include `is:saved` items.
- Note what didn't happen that was planned (Linear tickets carried over).

## Step 2: Week ahead
- Use Google Calendar to scan next week (load, conflicts, prep needed).
- Carry-over items from this week (Linear).
- Anything with a deadline in the next 10 days (Linear due dates, Calendar invites with prep).

## Step 3: Reflection prompt
One question: "What's the single thing that, if it happened next week, would make the week a win?" Record the answer for Friday's next run.

## Guidelines
- Don't recap for recap's sake. Lead with what changed.
- Flag stalled items explicitly — "no movement this week" is signal.
