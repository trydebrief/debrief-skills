# /debrief catchup

"I wasn't on the call — what do I need to know?" A tight brief for someone who needs to be caught up fast.

## Apps
- **Granola** — pull the transcript of the meeting the user missed.

## Step 1: Pull transcript
Use Granola to fetch the meeting the user missed. Confirm date and attendees.

## Step 2: Write the brief
Three sections, in order of importance to the absent person:
- **What was decided** (so they don't re-litigate)
- **What affects them** (action items assigned to them, decisions on their area)
- **What's worth knowing** (context, surprising moments, customer signal)

Keep the whole thing under 200 words unless the meeting was >60 min.

## Guidelines
- Assume they'll skim. Front-load the ask-of-them.
- Flag any moment where their absence was noted or a decision was deferred pending their input.
