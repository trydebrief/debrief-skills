# /debrief newhire

Assemble scattered info about someone joining the team into one pack.

## Apps
- **HubSpot** — candidate background if they came through pipeline.
- **Slack** — invitation, channel membership. Also check saved-for-later via `is:saved`.
- **Linear** — projects they're being assigned to.
- **Notion** — planning docs that mention them; the destination for the pack.

## Step 1: Identify
User names the new hire. Find them via:
- HubSpot (if candidate came through pipeline)
- Slack (if invited — include `is:saved` items that reference them)
- Linear (if assigned to projects)
- Notion (if mentioned in planning docs)

## Step 2: Build the pack
- Role and reporting line
- Background (from HubSpot / LinkedIn notes if present)
- What they'll work on (linked Linear projects)
- Who their close collaborators are (Slack channels, Linear teams)
- Any pre-start commitments made during hiring (HubSpot / Notion)

## Step 3: Save
Create the pack as a Notion page in the team space. Link from it. Notify the hiring manager via Slack draft that it's ready.

## Guidelines
- Scrub anything that was confidential to the hiring process (salary, competing offers, candidate comparisons).
