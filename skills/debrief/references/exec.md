# /debrief exec

Weekly 15-minute summary for a principal: what they need to know, decide, or attend to.

## Apps
- **Granola** — meetings the principal missed but should know about.
- **Slack** — escalations bubbling up; decisions awaiting input. Also check saved-for-later via `is:saved`.
- **Gmail** — escalations in email; decisions pending.
- **Linear** — milestones that moved.

## Step 1: Gather
- Use Granola to find recent meetings the principal missed but should know about.
- Use Slack and Gmail to find decisions awaiting their input and escalations bubbling up; in Slack, include `is:saved`.
- Use Linear to surface milestones that moved.

## Step 2: Structure
- **Decide**: items needing their sign-off, with a recommendation
- **Know**: context they should have, no action needed
- **Attend**: meetings/moments in the next week that need them

## Step 3: Deliver
Keep it under 400 words. Link out for depth (Linear projects, Slack permalinks, Granola transcripts).

## Guidelines
- Lead with Decide. If there's nothing to decide, say so — don't pad.
- Make a recommendation on every Decide item. "FYI" without a rec is lazy.
