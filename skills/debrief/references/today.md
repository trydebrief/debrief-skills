# /debrief today

Three things that actually matter today. Cut the rest.

## Apps
- **Google Calendar** — today's schedule and remaining open blocks.
- **Linear** — overdue tasks and active goal-linked tickets.
- **Notion** — active goals / initiatives.

## Step 1: Look at the inputs
- Use Google Calendar for today's events and remaining open blocks.
- Use Linear for overdue tasks and tickets tied to active goals.
- Use Notion for active goals/initiatives and commitments due this week.

## Step 2: Pick three
Criteria: high impact AND feasible today (given Calendar load). If a high-impact item isn't feasible, surface it separately as a "plan for" item.

## Step 3: Justify each
One sentence per pick on why it matters and what "done" looks like. Link the source (Linear ticket / Notion page).

## Step 4: Recommend cuts
Name 1–2 things on the plate that could slip to tomorrow without cost.

## Guidelines
- Three is a ceiling, not a target. If there's really only one, say one.
- Impact beats urgency. A merely urgent thing that doesn't move the needle shouldn't make the list.
