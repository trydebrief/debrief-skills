# /debrief owed

Who owes the user what, and how late it is.

## Apps
- **Slack** — search messages directed to the user for second-person commitments. Also check saved-for-later via `is:saved`.
- **Gmail** — search inbound mail for the same.
- **Granola** — search transcripts for verbal commitments others made to the user.

## Step 1: Scan (last 30 days)
Same sources as `/debrief promises`, but inverted — second-person commitments directed at the user: "I'll send you…", "I'll have that by…". Use Slack, Gmail, and Granola accordingly; include Slack `is:saved` items.

## Step 2: Resolve
For each, check inbound (Gmail / Slack DM): did that person send the thing? Mark resolved or still owed.

## Step 3: Present
Sort by overdue-ness. Include:
- Person
- What they promised
- When they promised it (link to source)
- How late (or how soon due)

Offer to draft a gentle nudge for the top 1–2 overdue items via Slack or Gmail (save as draft, never send).

## Guidelines
- Tone matters on nudges — draft in the user's voice, not a template.
- Don't nudge people on items <3 days overdue unless user asks.
