# /debrief teamup

Pull from PRs, tickets, and Slack to draft a team's weekly update.

## Apps
- **GitHub** — merged PRs per team member, with size/impact.
- **Linear** — tickets closed per team member.
- **Slack** — notable contributions (decisions, answers, unblocks). Also check saved-for-later via `is:saved`.

## Step 1: Scope the team
Ask which team (or infer from user's org). Identify members.

## Step 2: Aggregate per member
- Use GitHub to list merged PRs per member with size/impact.
- Use Linear to list tickets closed per member.
- Search Slack for notable contributions in team channels; include `is:saved` items the user bookmarked.

## Step 3: Draft
Structure:
- **Team highlights** (2–3 things worth calling out)
- **Shipped** (by person or by area, whichever reads cleaner)
- **In flight**
- **Risks / asks** (what the team needs from the broader org)

## Step 4: Confirm
Show the draft. Offer to post via Slack to a channel, or save to Notion. Don't post without confirmation.

## Guidelines
- Celebrate specific work, not effort. Named PRs > "worked hard this week".
- Risks section is often the most valuable — don't skip it.
