# /debrief deepwork

Find the longest uninterrupted stretch this week and assign the highest-leverage task to it.

## Apps
- **Google Calendar** — scan free/busy and create the focus block on confirm.

## Step 1: Scan calendar
Use Google Calendar to identify the longest contiguous meeting-free blocks across the week. Rank by length.

## Step 2: Match to task
Look at the user's priority list (ask if unsure). Pick the task that most benefits from long focused time (writing, deep coding, strategic thinking) and assign it to the longest block.

## Step 3: Propose the block
Suggest creating a Calendar event with a descriptive title. Confirm before creating via Google Calendar.

## Guidelines
- Protect the block from meetings — suggest a "focus" calendar color or status.
- Don't over-schedule. One deep block per day is plenty.
