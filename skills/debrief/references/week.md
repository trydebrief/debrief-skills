# /debrief week

Top priorities for the week with explicit reasoning, so the user can push back.

## Apps
- **Google Calendar** — week's load and conflicts.
- **Linear** — overdue items and upcoming deadlines.
- **Notion** — active goals and recent landscape changes.

## Step 1: Gather
- Use Google Calendar for the week's load and conflicts.
- Use Linear for overdue items and upcoming deadlines.
- Use Notion for active goals and any recent landscape changes.

## Step 2: Propose 3–5 priorities
For each:
- What it is (link to Linear ticket / Notion page)
- Why it's on the list (tie to a goal, deadline, or risk)
- What success looks like by Friday
- Rough effort estimate

## Step 3: Invite pushback
End with: "Does this ranking match your gut? Anything missing or mis-ordered?"

## Guidelines
- Show your work. The reasoning is the product.
- If two items conflict (can't both be done this week), say so explicitly.
