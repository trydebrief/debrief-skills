# /debrief mentions

Everywhere the user was @'d or tagged but hasn't responded.

## Apps
- **Slack** — search for @mentions of the user across public/private channels. Also check saved-for-later via `is:saved`.
- **Gmail** — search for messages addressed To the user (not just CC'd).

## Step 1: Scan (last 14 days)
- Search Slack for @mentions of the user across public/private channels; also check `is:saved` for bookmarked mentions.
- Search Gmail for threads where the user is on To: (not just CC) and didn't reply.

## Step 2: Per mention
- Source (Slack channel/thread permalink, or Gmail thread)
- Who tagged them
- What they asked
- Age

## Step 3: Present
Sort by age. Offer to draft replies for the top 3–5 (Slack `send_message_draft` or Gmail `create_draft`).

## Guidelines
- Include mentions in archived/muted channels — being muted doesn't mean the ask is less valid.
