# /debrief relate

Haven't talked to X in a while — here's what they've been up to and a suggested reach-out.

## Apps
- **Gmail** — last email thread with the person.
- **Granola** — last meeting transcript with them.
- **Slack** — last shared message or DM. Also check saved-for-later via `is:saved`.

## Step 1: Identify
User names the person. Use Gmail, Granola, and Slack to find their last interaction across each; include Slack `is:saved` items that reference this person.

## Step 2: Gather public signal
- Their last message or meeting (Gmail / Granola / Slack)
- Any outstanding commitment the user owes them (cross-check with `/debrief promises`)

## Step 3: Suggest a reach-out
Draft a short, specific note that references something real — the email they last sent, the meeting topic, or the lapsed commitment. Save as a Gmail draft or Slack draft. Not "just checking in".

## Guidelines
- Specific beats frequent. Don't suggest reach-outs without a real hook.
- If there's an outstanding commitment on the user's side, lead with that.
